People in developed countries with established healthcare systems tend to be most willing to donate blood. Saudi Arabia ,India ,China and many European countries have high rates of blood donation.

The number of blood banks per 10 lakh people varies among Indian states, with some states having over 8 blood banks per 10 lakh people and others having less than 1. The availability of blood banks is crucial for ensuring a safe and adequate supply of blood for transfusions.
